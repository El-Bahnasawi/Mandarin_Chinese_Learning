Chinese Radicals 🀄

汉字部首 — 59 Common Radicals, Categorized

👶 Beginner friendly · Emoji hints · Example sentences

Radicals are the building blocks of Chinese characters. They often hint at the meaning. Here are 59 radicals grouped by theme. Use the emojis to remember them! 🎯
